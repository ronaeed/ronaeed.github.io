Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Percentage?

A percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100, and it is denoted using the percent sign, '%'.

How to Calculate a Percentage?

To calculate a percentage, divide the part by the whole and then multiply by 100. For example, if 20 out of 50 apples are red, the percentage of red apples can be calculated as follows: (20/50) * 100 = 40%. So, 40% of the apples are red.

How to Calculate Percentage Increase?

To calculate percentage increase, first determine the difference between the two numbers you are comparing. Divide this difference by the original number and then multiply by 100. For example, if a product's price increases from $50 to $60, the percentage increase is calculated as (($60 - $50) / $50) * 100 = 20%.
